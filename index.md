# Hi, I'm Simone Cammarasana

I am a Researcher (III level) at [CNR-IMATI](https://www.imati.cnr.it/), Genova, Italy,
where I work on signal and image processing, deep learning, and biomedical imaging.
My research focuses on developing innovative methods for the analysis and enhancement
of ultrasound and MRI data, with applications to neurological and oncological diseases.

<a class="btn-cv" href="/assets/CV.pdf" download>Download CV (PDF)</a>

## Research Interests

**Super-Resolution & Denoising**: Design of learning-based and quality-preserving methods for
spatial and temporal super-resolution and denoising of 2D/3D ultrasound images and MRI volumes,
including real-time processing pipelines validated in clinical settings.

**Biomedical Image Analysis**: Processing of multimodal data (MRI T1, fMRI, PET, CT, ultrasound)
for neurological and oncological applications, with a focus on quantitative biomarker extraction
and support to preventive, predictive, and personalised medicine.

**Signal Sampling & Approximation**: Kernel-based and Fuzzy-transform methods for sampling,
reconstruction, and approximation of arbitrary 1D/2D/3D signals and biomedical images.

**Graph-based Learning**: Graph representation and learning techniques for drug administration
prediction, simulation of radiotracer concentration in neurological diseases, and analysis
of heterogeneous clinical data (EHR).

**High-Performance Computing**: Parallel and distributed algorithms on HPC clusters (CINECA
Marconi, Leonardo) for solving large-scale sparse linear systems and training deep learning models.
